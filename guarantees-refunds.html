Guarantee and Refund Policy

1. Game Key Assurance

Every digital item purchased from PQuickTacticsPlay.com.com comes with a key activation guarantee. This ensures that your game key is authentic, valid, and redeemable on the intended platform (e.g., Steam, Epic Games, Battle.net).

Should you face any problems activating your key, please reach out to our support team promptly through the Contact page.

2. Refund Conditions

Given the digital nature of our products, refunds are typically not available after a game key has been delivered and redeemed. However, we evaluate refund requests individually if the product was not received or fails to activate.

If an error on our end occurs (e.g., receiving an incorrect key), we will fully cover the cost of a refund or provide a replacement.

3. Refund Procedure

Once a refund is authorized, the amount will be credited back within 5–14 business days, depending on your payment method. Refunds will be processed using the original payment method, unless otherwise arranged.

4. Game Swaps

To exchange a game for a different title, contact our support team prior to redeeming the key. Exchanges are possible only if the key remains unused and the desired replacement is in stock.

5. Get in Touch

For any inquiries about guarantees, refunds, or key activation, please reach out to our support team via the Contact page. Our team is available to assist you every day of the week.
