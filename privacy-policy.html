Data Privacy Policy

1. Overview

This policy outlines how we manage personal information for users who visit or purchase from PlayRewardLegends.com. We are dedicated to safeguarding your privacy and handle all data in compliance with relevant regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Information We Gather

When you make a purchase or create an account on our site, we may request the following details:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Contact number
  • Delivery address

These details are essential for processing orders and providing customer assistance.

3. Use of Collected Data

We use your personal information for the following reasons:

  • To process and deliver user orders
  • To communicate with users about their purchases
  • To share news, promotions, and offers if you’ve opted into our newsletter
  • To enhance site performance and analyze user behavior

4. Data Security

We implement robust technical and organizational measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, modification, or loss. Only authorized staff bound by confidentiality agreements can access user information.

5. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • View your personal information
  • Update, remove, or restrict the use of your data
  • Revoke consent at any time
  • Transfer your data to another service provider
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

6. Policy Updates

We may revise this privacy policy as needed. Any changes will be posted on this page and take effect immediately. We recommend checking this policy periodically to stay updated on how we protect your information.

7. Get in Touch

For any inquiries about this privacy policy, please reach out via the Contact page.
