Shipping Information

Shipping Methods

At QuickTacticsPlay.com.com, we offer reliable shipping services within Canada and to select international destinations. Available shipping options include:

  • Canada Post Standard Delivery (3–7 business days)
  • Canada Post Xpresspost (1–3 business days)
  • International Shipping (7–14 business days, depending on the destination)

Shipping fees are calculated automatically at checkout based on the chosen method and delivery location.

Order Monitoring

After your order is shipped, you’ll receive a tracking number to follow your package’s progress in real time via Canada Post or our international shipping partners.
